West Point Brewing

West Point Brewing is a brewery based in Vancouver with the unique selling point being a brewery that sells beer, sours, and cider infused with locally sourced ingredients such as blackberries, cedar, etc.

My task was to create a logo as well as associated packaging. My solution for it was to represent the act of people harvesting the local ingredients and the following process of the ingredients getting infused into the beverage in a fun, illustrative way.

Brand Identity
Packaging
Logo Development
Social Media Motion Reel

The logo takes the imagery of a beer tap and creates a text-based logo with hand-done type. This creates imagery that could also work as a physical beer tap to be implemented in other breweries.

With the unique process West Point Brewing employs to create its beverages. My favourite concept to bring to full fidelity was to make the process and the packaging be one and the same.

All food items are named after streets on ‘The Drive,’ celebrating the area’s people, homes, and community spirit.

To tie it all together, I created a social media reel to present the unique process and advertise the locations of the brewery. The video is sized for Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube shorts and allows space for margins of text that are automatically displayed on social media accounts to not get in front of the video.

Social media these days is by far the most effective way to reach a targeted, but massive audience easily and get the most eyes on an expanding business.

I made a series of three different beverage packaging sets, but the front cover varies in order to have distinct front-facing packaging to distinguish between the different beverages.

For the bottles, I wanted to keep the labels consistent with the appearance of the packaging, consistent in style, but distinct by a range of colours that could be applied to a myriad of drinks.
