doze

Doze is a snack brand that cares about two things: great sleep and great food. Around the world, 1 in 3 people—2.6 billion—struggle with insomnia, often influenced by what they snack on before bed.

They solve this with snacks as delicious as your favourites, but free of ingredients that keep you awake. Instead, they use reduced sugar and sodium, plus natural sleep-supporting ingredients like melatonin and magnesium, having your head hit the pillow in no time.

The brand needed a full brand identity, a logo, packaging for their snacks, along with secondary packaging to match. Finally, to get the word out they needed social media promotion, and a web presence to find out more info.

i. brand guide

For the logo, soft flowy letterforms harking back to freeform psychadelic lettering gives the logo a soft, dreamy appearance. The 'Z' takes the classic onomatopoeia of 'ZZZ' making the overall logo immedietly tied to sleep.

As for colours, the primary leading colour is a deep purple, which has deep rooted connotations with slumber and feeling calm. From multiple moodboard options, the colours and overall identity lead to the visual theme of space and the cosmos, where it is eternally nighttime, and the stars surround you.

ii. Packaging

The packaging builds a complete space scene with a planet, orbiting moons and stars. Each planet uses a coloured see-through film that reveals the snacks inside, reinforcing Doze’s transparency around natural ingredients and the snacks you eat. The orbiting moons double as callouts for protein, sugar and fat content, highlighting the snacks’ health benefits while enhancing the space theme.

iii. Secondary packaging for every location

For secondary packaging, Doze needed both individual shelf packs and custom aisle stands. If done right, secondary packaging becomes a valuable touchpoint — and for Doze, it’s also a way to expand their presence throughout the store.

Because Doze is a wellness snack designed for better sleep, it can sit in multiple store locations: the snack aisle as a healthier alternative, the health-food section alongside other healthy snacks, and even the pharmacy as a natural replacement to sleep supplements.

iv. Social Media motion

In a sea of competition, Doze needs to stand out through bold advertising that turns heads. A social media concept was devised where the protagonist struggling to sleep is visited by an extra-terrestrial stranger from another world, to tie in with the space theme.

v. finally, if you want to learn more

If you're still hungry for more, on the back of every package lies a call to action and URL leading the user to a landing page. Here you can learn a little more about what Doze is all about, their brand mission, and learn a bit more about how they actually make these sleepy snacks.
