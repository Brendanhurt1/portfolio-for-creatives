Zokusha Workshop

Based on the insight that most custom shops in Vancouver lack defined personality, Zokusha Workshop is not just a car custom shop bursting with character, but also a space where clients work alongside staff to build their dream vehicle.

The task was to brand a complete set of consistent deliverables so that Zokusha Workshop can thrive. Based on the rebellious culture of street racing in Japan, Zokusha takes aspects of the neon-lit city life in Japan to bring innovative JDM customs to Vancouver.
