Frank's Redhot

Frank's RedHot is an iconic brand and North America's most successful Hot Sauce brand, dating back to 1928. While I can't argue that the sauce is delicious and versatile with their slogan literally stating "I Put That S**t on Everything!", I believe their branding could use a little modernizing while keeping some of the motifs that make it recognizable in the first place.

Brand Identity
Logo Design
Packaging

Frank’s logo has subtle charm—like the symmetrical ‘dH’—but since it’s “Frank’s” RedHot, it needed a more personal script to reflect its handcrafted roots. I refined the kerning and added a flame to the ‘o,’ giving it character without losing simplicity.

By cropping down images to a tight angle and combining a refined colour palette, I made three solid directions for the brand to go in. While some shouldn’t stray too far from the brand’s current vision, it never hurts to present ideas that are outside of the box with a solid vision to back them.

After review, we settled on the ‘Bold’ look for the final colour scheme and the branding for Frank’s new appearance.

For the bottles, I refreshed core elements and developed three concepts adaptable across Frank’s full product line.

To express heat and flavor, I set the wordmark in a bold flame graphic, using subtle texture instead of dated effects for a modern, heritage-inspired look.
