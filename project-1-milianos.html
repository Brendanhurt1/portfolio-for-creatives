Miliano's Restaurant

Miliano’s is a restaurant set in the heart of Commercial Drive’s Little Italy. Here, every walk of life is welcome to get a true taste of community and Italian culture. Based on the idea of family- oriented long table dining, large tables serve as the perfect place to meet a new friend with a stranger you’ve never met.

My objective was to entirely brand a guide to Miliano’s visual identity through a brand guide, menu, as well as collateral pieces, including packaging and more.
