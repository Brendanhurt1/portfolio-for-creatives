Miliano's Restaurant

Miliano’s is a restaurant set in the heart of Commercial Drive’s Little Italy. Here, every walk of life is welcome to get a true taste of community and Italian culture. Based on the idea of family- oriented long table dining, large tables serve as the perfect place to meet a new friend with a stranger you’ve never met.

My objective was to entirely brand a guide to Miliano’s visual identity through a brand guide, menu, as well as collateral pieces, including packaging and more.

The logo, featuring the national bird of Italy, the Italian Sparrow, is a very social bird. Featured inside of a family crest, the logo represents the meeting of new faces but also the importance of family.

The logo, featuring the national bird of Italy, the Italian Sparrow, is a very social bird. Featured inside of a family crest, the logo represents the meeting of new faces but also the importance of family.

The menu uses Italian mosaic tile shapes with graphic textures from photos taken on Commercial Drive. Every wall, brick, and mural is reflected in the design, with each menu color-coded for easy distinction. Brand characters appear throughout, representing specific dishes.

All food items are named after streets on ‘The Drive,’ celebrating the area’s people, homes, and community spirit.

Mosaic tile style coasters serve as a perfect complement to the menu, featuring a consistent brand motif of the tiles representing the eclectic appearance of Commercial Drive.

The in-restaurant pasta sauce trio brings together unexpected ingredient pairings that work perfectly—just like the unlikely people sharing the long table, where your perfect pairing might be a stranger across from you.
