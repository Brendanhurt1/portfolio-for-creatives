• Hello!

    I'm Brendan, a branding designer who infuses my passion into hard work and getting down to business. Although brand design is my pride and joy, I like to cast a wide net, and I particularly love packaging, motion design, and campaign work. Born and raised in Vancouver, I started my design journey earning a Certificate of Design from BCIT and moved on to study at Capilano University’s IDEA program.

    As a self-described pop culture nerd, I get inspired by every medium of artistic expression and storytelling, whether it be through film, music, or other design work I see out in the world. I’m constantly drawing inspiration from classic tropes from movies, to compelling songwriting, and album covers. After all, elegant design is nothing without a great narrative to back it. As designers, we have the privilege of being able to pull inspiration to inform our work from just about anywhere, solving real-world problems we all face every day.
