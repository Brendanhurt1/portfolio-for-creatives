• Hello!

    My name is Brendan Hurt, and I’m a graphic designer who infuses my passion into hard work and getting down to business. While brand design is my pride and joy, I like to cast a wide net, and I particularly love packaging, motion design, and campaign work. Born and raised in Vancouver, I started my design journey earning a Certificate of Design from BCIT and moved on to study at Capilano University’s IDEA program.

    As a self-described pop culture nerd, I love just about every branching subgenre of pop and rock music, and I could ramble on for hours about how I’m inspired by the whole filmography of a director. What I love about graphic design is that you can get inspired by every form of the artistic process, and I’m continually inspired to push boundaries while solving problems we all face every day.
