    My name is Brendan Hurt, and I'm a graphic designer proficient in branding, digital illustration and motion design, born and raised in Vancouver. I started my design journey earning a Certificate of Design from BCIT and went on to study at Capilano University as part of the IDEA program.

    I certainly would describe myself as a pop culture nerd, and I'm endlessly inspired by the themes and imagery of film and music. What I love about graphic design is that every variation of the creative process is deeply intertwined, and I’m continually inspired to push boundaries while solving problems we all face every day.
