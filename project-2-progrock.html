Prog Rock Odyssey

The concept for Prog Rock Odyssey was to take the retro sound systems from the era of progressive rock, alongside the otherworldly themes of space and time and create a poster for a progressive rock festival that turns into a working interactive sound system.

The festival needed a visual identity reflecting the sounds of the era, but also a poster that used the typical format for a festival lineup and flipped it on its head. To propell promotion for the festival, social media advertising and a unique interactive portion of the poster tie it all together.

Hypothetical Concept

i. A Poster that tells a story

For the poster particularly, I wanted the components from sound systems of Progressive Rock, like synthesizers, to act as the poster information such as the artist lineup and event date.

ii. An AR Experience with Social Media To Back it

The real star of the show is the AR poster, which truly brings the concept of the poster to life. The interaction can be placed on any wall using a mobile device and with the tap of a button, the comes alive, giving the user the ability to preview the artists performing at the show.

Finally, using the assets built into the poster, I created a simple social media promotion in Adobe After Effects. The video is quick, to the point, and visually aligns with the poster.
