Prog Rock Odyssey

The concept for Prog Rock Odyssey was to take the retro sound systems from the era of progressive rock, alongside the otherworldly themes of space and time and create a poster for a progressive rock festival that turns into a working interactive sound system.

Brand Guide
Menu Design
Packaging
Collateral Pieces

For the poster particularly, I wanted the retro sound system components, like early synthesizers, to act as the poster information such as the artist lineup and event date.

The logo, featuring the national bird of Italy, the Italian Sparrow, is a very social bird. Featured inside of a family crest, the logo represents the meeting of new faces but also the importance of family.
